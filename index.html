Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our photo editing service.

How do I place an order?

Simply browse our editing styles, click "Order on WhatsApp" on your chosen style, and send us your photo with any specific instructions. We'll confirm your order and begin editing.

How long will editing take?

Standard delivery time is 24-48 hours. For urgent requests, we offer express service (12 hours) for an additional fee. We'll provide an estimated delivery time when you place your order.

Which format should I send my photo in?

We accept JPG, PNG, and RAW files. For best results, send the highest quality version available. For printing purposes, we recommend sending RAW files if possible.

Can I request changes after you send the edit?

Yes! We offer one round of minor revisions for free. After receiving the preview, you can request adjustments. Major changes or completely different editing styles may require an additional fee.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer refunds if we're unable to deliver the service as described. If you're not satisfied with the edit, we'll work with you to make it right. Refunds are not offered for completed work that matches the ordered style.

Will you use my photos for posts or ads?

No, we never use customer photos for marketing without explicit permission. Your photos remain private and are used only for editing. We may ask to use an edit as a sample in our gallery, but only with your consent.
